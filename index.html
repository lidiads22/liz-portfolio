About Me

Hi, I’m Elizabeth De Santiago — a Mortgage Broker and community advocate driven by a heart for service. My journey into mortgage lending began with a deep desire to help families like mine feel confident and informed when making one of life’s biggest decisions: buying a home.

Outside of lending, I’m proud to lead financial literacy workshops across San Antonio, empower Spanish-speaking communities, and partner with schools and nonprofits to break financial barriers. Whether I’m hosting a workshop at Empower House or sharing insights at Edison High School, I believe knowledge is the first step to generational wealth.

Every day, I aim to bring care, clarity, and compassion to the homebuying process. Because for me, it’s more than numbers — it’s about people, families, and dreams.
